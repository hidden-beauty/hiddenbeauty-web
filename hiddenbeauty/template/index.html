Welcome to Hidden Beauty!

The Hidden Beauty project aims to allow people to view other humans and to show that We Are Beautiful. The human body takes many shapes and forms and we should all be comfortable in our own bodies. Modern society puts an incredible amount of pressure on us to look a certain way. We are constantly bombarded with images of attractive people and we start to think that we are somehow inadequate.

Model 000000-HSNN

We are not inadequate; we're merely human and come in many lovely shapes and sizes. If you've ever wonderer if you are normal or are curious about the human body, welcome to Hidden Beauty. This site allows you to explore our models in great detail in the privacy of your own home. You can download any of these models and print them using a 3D printer or you can even have a print made for you.

We suggest that you to start with our exhibits for the best introduction to this site, or browse our models online, download the 3D solids for printing or order a print online. You may also download the surfaces and use them in your own 3D modeling projects.

We have a number of ways to browse this site:

browse by body part: All models organized by body part.

browse by model: All models organized by human model.

browse by attributes: Browse models based on their attributes.

browse by date: Browse models based on their creation date.
