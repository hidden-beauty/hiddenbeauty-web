Welcome to Hidden Beauty!

The Hidden Beauty project aims to allow people to view other humans and to show that We Are Beautiful. The human body takes many shapes and forms and we should all be comfortable in our own bodies and have a positive body image. However, modern society puts an incredible amount of pressure on us to look a certain way. We are constantly bombarded with a very narrow range of body types which are held up as a beauty ideal and we may start to think that we are somehow inadequate.

We are not inadequate; we're merely human and come in many lovely shapes and sizes. And that is just fine, because we are all beautiful! ♥

If you've ever wondered if you are normal or are curious about the human body, welcome to Hidden Beauty. This site allows you to explore our models in great detail in privacy. You can view the models online or download any of these models, whether to use them for new projects or to print them using a 3D printer. If you don't have access to a 3D printer, you may also order prints online from this site. All the models and content on this site are released into the public domain, which means you are free to use anything on this site as you see fit.
